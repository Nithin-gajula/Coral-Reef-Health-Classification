About Coral Health Classification

This project aims to classify the health status of coral reefs using deep learning techniques. Coral reefs are vital ecosystems that support marine life and provide coastal protection. However, climate change and human activities pose significant threats to coral health.

Our model uses advanced deep learning architectures to predict whether a given coral image is "Healthy" or "Bleached." The system is designed to assist marine researchers and conservationists in monitoring coral health effectively.

The application is user-friendly, allowing users to upload images and obtain quick and accurate health predictions. We strive to support coral conservation efforts through technology.
